Capsule Network for Category-level 6D Object Pose Estimation

Description:

Category-level object pose estimation networks outperform instance-level 6d pose estimation networks in terms of supporting objects [1] and get more and more attention in the computer vision community. Objects in the same category have similar structure, which provides vital information for object detection and estimation of 6d poses [2]. Capsule networks [3] have the advantage of better understanding the relationship of different object components and avoid false positives. In this work, the student should utilize capsule network structure in category-level object 6d pose estimations for better explainability and higher accuracy.

Requirement:

Students should be familiar with pytorch or tensorflow and have hands-on experience with deep learning in past projects. Aiming for a paper publication would be a plus.

[1] Manhardt, Fabian, et al. “CPS++: Improving Class-level 6D Pose and Shape Estimation From Monocular Images With Self-Supervised Learning.” arXiv preprint arXiv:2003.05848 (2020).

[2] Chen, Kai, and Qi Dou. “Sgpa: Structure-guided prior adaptation for category-level 6d object pose estimation.” Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF International Conference on Computer Vision. 2021.

[3]Lalonde, Rodney, Naji Khosravan, and Ulas Bagci. “Deformable Capsules for Object Detection.” arXiv preprint arXiv:2104.05031 (2021).
